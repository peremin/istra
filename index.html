Apartment house in a calm and secluded area.

This holiday home is situated in a peaceful part of Betiga, near Barbariga in the southern part of the Istrian peninsula, opposite the Brijuni archipelago.

The proximity of pebble beaches, Fazana, the National Park of Brijuni and the towns Pula and Rovinj will make your holiday even more interesting.


If you are interested in cultural heritage, it is good to know that Barbariga received its name after the Venetian noble family Barbarigo, who owned properties in this region. By the old Roman road below the village Batvaci, the Basilica of St. Foska, dating from the 6th-7th century, decorated with frescos from the 12th century, one of the oldest in Istria.
